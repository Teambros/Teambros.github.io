Tips on the food to include in your diet to prevent diabetes

foods that should be included in your diet to reduce risks of diabetes

Posted by

Brief

Diabetes is one of the metabolic diseases whereby the level of blood sugar in the body rises due to inadequate production of insulin in the body or when the body cells fail to respond to the insulin. Apparently, this disease prevalence is very high. For instance, about 9% of the total population in the USA is living with this disease. However, findings show that the choices you make about what goes into your body contribute greatly in either reducing or increasing the risk of getting diabetes. Below are some of the food that should be included in your diet to reduce this risk.

1. Beans

Beans are known to contain fiber which is necessary in preventing blood sugars from rising. Moreover, beans contain some protein which is necessary in the body which is better than proteins from red meat which contains a lot of cholesterol. This means by including some beans in your diet, you will get the necessary proteins required for optimal health while reducing the risks of getting diabetes.

2. Green leafy vegetables

All the green Vegas like spinach and kale must be included in the diet. These Vegas are rich in fiber, Vitamin, C and calcium. Fiber and calcium plays a major role in lowering the fat content in the body which lowers the blood sugars.

3. Water

It is recommended that a person should take between six to eight glasses of water each day. Unfortunately, we like taking water when we get thirsty. A research that was conducted in France shows that people who take a lot of water have got high low chances of getting diabetes and other metabolic diseases than those who don’t. Water plays a major role in excretion and regulation of the blood sugars.

4. Barley

Barley is also rich in fiber and calcium which are necessary in prevention of high blood sugars. This means if you incorporate some barley in your diet, you lower the cholesterol which contributes to heart diseases that in return contribute to inadequate production of insulin. Barley should be taken as a grain and not from the processed food.

5. Diary products

Dairy products like milk, yogurt and cheese contain some Vitamin D and calcium. A study was done that shows that people who take calcium and vitamin D together daily have low chances of becoming diabetic than those who don’t. In fact, people who are diabetic are advised to take yogurt more often. These nutrients in the dairy products help in lowering the fat content in the body.

6. Apple

Apple has a substance which is called quercetin. Quercetin has inflammatory and antioxidant effects which are used to prevent heart disease lower the level of cholesterol in the body and strengthen the arteries. These functions are enough to lower your chances of becoming diabetic. In fact, quercetin is used in the treatment of diabetes- Therefore, even those people with diabetes should take apples so that they can lower the level of their blood sugars.

7. Citrus Fruit

This fruit is rich in Vitamin C. The main nutrients that are responsible for prevention of diabetes are Vitamin 0, fiber, calcium and Vitamin D. Some prefer getting vitamin C from multivitamin drugs. However, there is no need to spend cash on drugs while you can get it from such a fruit. in addition, this fruit contains fiber which will boost the Vitamin C in prevention of diabetes.

8. Berries

Berries are fruits which are very rich in nutrients. They contain fiber, Vitamin C, folic acid and phytochemicals. Studies have shown that they contribute greatly to prevention of cardiovascular disease. One of the phytochemicals is polyphenols which is an antioxidant which enhances the functionality of the heart thus maintaining good cholesterol level. When all these nutrients are taken together from one product, they are very effective in prevention of diabetes as well as treatment.

9. Green tea

Green tea is packed with flavonoids which is a powerful inflammation fighter. Chronic inflammations develop in the heart which may lead to heart attack thereby affecting the absorption of blood sugars in the body.

However, flavonoids from green tea normally fight these inflammations and in return prevent rise in the body sugars. This makes green tea good for prevention of diabetes.

10. Nuts

Nuts are known to be high calorie food and hence most people relate them to weight gain and hence they avoid them. However, if taken in regulated manner, nuts can play a major role in prevention of diabetes. There are studies to show that nuts lower the risks of heart disease thus being effective in prevention of diabetes. People who eat nuts are said to be healthier than those who don’t. Most people eat nuts inform of peanut butter but it is advised to eat it unprocessed to increase the chances of preventing diabetes.

11. Lentils

Lentils are similar to beans. They are very rich in fiber, proteins, vitamins and minerals. All these nutrients are responsible for the prevention of diabetes because they make the body to have steady blood sugars. Lentils have other health benefits and hence should be included in the diet.

12. Dark chocolate

Dark Chocolate has an antioxidant that helps in improving the cholesterol in the body as well maintaining good blood pressure. Good level of cholesterol and blood pressure translates to lower chances of getting diabetes.

13. Cold water fish

Cold water fish has omega 3 fats which are known to provide good cholesterol in the body thus reducing the chances of heart disease. As discussed, heart disease contributes to development of diabetes and hence taking omega 3 fats will help in preventing it.

14. Oat

Oat is very similar to beans and lentils as it is also rich in proteins and fiber. Therefore, it is as effective as the beans and the lentils.

15. Vinegar

2 tablespoons of vinegar everyday are can greatly reduce the blood sugars in your body. This means vinegar can help in preventing diabetes as well as treating it since it will help in maintaining steady level of blood sugars.

Therefore, what you take every moment can greatly determine if you will have optimal health or otherwise. You should, therefore, strive to take what is of benefit to the body like the above food that has been discussed so that you can prevent yourself from diseases which are avoidable. What you take can act as medicine or as a poison. Mind your diet.
