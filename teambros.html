Who we are


Teambros took its first step in Sydney in 2009 as a sole trading personal training team, and is now one of the best body transformation and personal training teams in Sydney. With continuous interest and strive to assist in achieving the best results in each and every one of our members, the Teambros team has proudly achieved a 100% weight loss guarantee and body transformation success rate for many years, now Teambros became synonymous with weight loss, nutrition or total body transformation through building its brand and incurring brand value in the eyes of our customers.

Teambros training team consists of professionals in chiropractic, exercise therapy, Pilates, and personal training. Professional knowledge in each of these four areas play a great advantage in recognizing and helping with problems that each member may have, with a training program personalized to suit each individual. Teambros is planning for their first health club to be situated in the heart of Sydney CBD and they will provide best health and fitness services based on their rich experience and knowledge.

Teambros director, Yohan Lim, has over 15 years of experience in health and fitness fields, over the past 10 years he has held number of positions including South Korea national strength & conditioning trainer at 2008 Beijing Paralympics and national training coach in 2007 Taiwan IWAS National Games. He has also served as a researcher at SAMSUNG laboratory of motor and movement development, experienced physiotherapeutic exercise and rehabilitation treatments, during the period, he has grafted these knowledge on fitness exercise and completed his current exercise theories, it increased his competitiveness in fitness field.
